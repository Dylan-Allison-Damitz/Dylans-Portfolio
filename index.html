About Me

My name is Dylan Allison-Damitz, I am currently 31 years old and I live in Hillsboro, Oregon with my wife of 6 years, Lark, and my 13-month old daughter, Cora. We have a golden-doodle named Willow.

I graduated from Portland State University with a bachelor's degree in English, with additional studies focused heavily on philosophy. After graduating from Portland State I went through the application process to become a police officer at Portland Police Bureau. I was hired and sworn in with Portland Police Bureau (PPB) on August 23rd, 2017. After 3 years spent on patrol I made the decision to shift away from police work and focus on a career field that had long captured both my heart and imagination: technology. I've always had an affinity for technology, science, and progress, in general. Learning how to code for the purpose of creating websites and software was a skill I'd longed for after taking some coding classes in high school. After reading about Epicodus during some late-night Googling, I felt compelled to try my hand at becoming a programmer. As I move forward into this field, I'm confident that my work and life experience, as well as my passion for words, syntax, and creating things, should catalyze a rewarding journey into this

I enjoy the outdoors, spending time with my family, playing and watching sports of any kind, listening to music, and working out.
